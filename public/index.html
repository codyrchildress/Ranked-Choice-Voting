Runoff ranked-choice voting

Second choices count.

Runoff holds ranked elections for your team, club, or friend group. Everyone ranks their favorites; you choose how the ballots are counted — instant-runoff, points, head-to-head, and more — and the results show what every method would have decided.

  1. 1
    Set up the ballot Name the election, add the options, pick the counting method, and choose how many ranked choices each voter gets.
  2. 2
    Share one link Anyone with the voter link casts one ballot — no accounts, no sign-ins.
  3. 3
    Close to reveal Tallies stay sealed until you close voting. Then everyone sees the official count — and what every other method would have decided.
